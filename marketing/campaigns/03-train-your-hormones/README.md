# Campaign 3 — Train Your Hormones Like You Train Your Body

> PowerHouse Gym members, gym-floor + social. Implements marketing
> [`GOLD.md`](../../GOLD.md) §4 (PowerHouse segment). Anchors TRT Dashboard to
> the PowerHouse brand: *data discipline applied to hormone health*.

---

## Strategy

**Audience:** PowerHouse Gym members — men ~25–55 who already track lifts,
macros, and sleep, and who may be on or considering TRT under a clinician.

**Core promise (compliant):** Apply the same data discipline you bring to
training to your labs — one timeline, real trends, a report your clinician can
actually use. **Not** a dose, **not** a diagnosis, **not** a "boost your T" pitch.

**What we do NOT promise:** a performance outcome, a physique transformation
tied to the product, or any start/stop/change of medication (GOLD §2.1).

## Channels & format

- **Primary:** PowerHouse gym floor (QR posters, screen loops), IG (Reels +
  static), local member email.
- **Secondary:** X, YouTube Shorts.
- **Format:** 6 scenes, vertical 9:16 (~30s) + 3 static key-art posts.
  Burned-in captions. AA contrast. Dark + light safe.

## KPIs

- QR scans on gym floor ≥ 200/campaign.
- IG Reel view-through ≥ 35%.
- Member sign-ups attributed ≥ 10% of QR scans.
- Zero §2 violations in creative review.

## Compliance notes

- Closing scene (Scene 6) carries the on-screen disclaimer; **full disclaimer**
  in every post caption and email footer.
- **No before/after physique transformations** attributed to the product
  (GOLD §2.1.6). Show data timelines and training-log parallels.
- Frame TRT as **data discipline**, not optimization hype. No steroid-adjacent
  imagery or "bro-T" copy.
- Actor is *organizing lab data*, not being diagnosed or dosed.
- Check IG/TikTok current health-ad policy before boosting.
