## Strategy

**Audience:** TRT patients — men ~30–55 already in (or evaluating) testosterone
therapy, sitting on years of fragmented lab PDFs from different labs and units.

**Core promise (compliant):** *Organize* your labs into a clean timeline and a
clinician-ready report — so your next visit starts with data, not a PDF hunt.

**What we do NOT promise:** a dose, a diagnosis, a lab-result outcome, or any
start/stop/change of medication (GOLD §2.1).

## Channels & format

- **Primary:** IG Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts (9:16 vertical, ~30s).
- **Secondary:** Reddit r/Testosterone (text + link), X short clip.
- **Format:** 6 scenes. Burned-in captions. AA contrast. Dark + light safe.

## KPIs

- Video view-through rate ≥ 35%.
- CTA click → landing page `https://trt.powerhousegym.co` ≥ 3% of views.
- Sign-ups attributed to campaign ≥ 5% of landing clicks.
- Zero §2 violations in creative review.

## Compliance notes

- Closing scene (Scene 6) carries the on-screen disclaimer; **full disclaimer**
  in every video description.
- No before/after medical transformations. Show **data timelines**, not bodies.
- Actor is *organizing data*, never *being diagnosed*.
- "Trend" and "report" language only — never "optimize," "fix," or "boost your T."
- Check IG/TikTok/YouTube current health-ad policy before boosting.
